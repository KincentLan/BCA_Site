Application

The Essential Step to a Bigger Goal

  • Application Process

    In order to apply successfully to this school, students must complete their online applications and take the entrance test. Each application is dealt with care and thought. After reviewing these, the school will then send out mail to the applicants who are deemed qualified for an interview. Afterward acceptance letters should be mailed out to allow students to know if they can attend the school.

  • Which Academy Should I Choose?

    Students are only able to pick one Academy. Each academy contains classes that are not usually available to other academies. Because of this, students are encouraged to choose an academy that seems to pursue his own passions or interests. Granted, almost any research, projects, and electives will be available to any academy.

    For more information about the academies, please click this here.

  • Student Requirements

    There are a specific set of requirements that any student applying to school must. They are as follows: Students must reside in Bergen County. Only 8th grade students can apply. No transfers. If the student is accepted, he/she will begin 9th grade in September. Transferring to BCA is not permitted.
